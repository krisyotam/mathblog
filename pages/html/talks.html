Introduction to Topology: Connected Spaces

TOPOLOGY • MAY 2024 • ENGLISH

An exploration of connected spaces in topology, covering the fundamental concepts and their applications in modern mathematics. This talk focuses on basic definitions, examples, and key theorems.

Abstract Algebra: Group Theory Fundamentals

ALGEBRA • APRIL 2024 • ENGLISH

A comprehensive introduction to group theory, exploring basic definitions, subgroups, normal subgroups, and homomorphisms. The talk includes visual demonstrations and practical examples.

Real Analysis: Sequences and Series

ANALYSIS • MARCH 2024 • ENGLISH

An in-depth look at sequences and series in real analysis, covering convergence tests, power series, and their applications. This talk bridges the gap between calculus and advanced analysis.
